IEEE RAS Chapter - Vice Chair

IEEE RAS (Robotics and Automation Society) is a society of IEEE that promotes the advancement of the theory and practice of robotics and automation engineering. Serving as the Vice Chair of the IEEE RAS Chapter, I actively contribute to organizing events, workshops, and activities related to robotics and automation. This role allows me to network with experts in the field and stay updated on the latest trends and innovations.
